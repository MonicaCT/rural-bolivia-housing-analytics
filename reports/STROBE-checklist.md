# STROBE cross-sectional reporting map

This map documents where the public methodological paper addresses major STROBE reporting items. It
does not claim that synthetic data constitute an observational study or that the original field study
is fully STROBE compliant.

| 1. Study design in title/abstract | Title, structured abstract | Complete |
| 2. Background and rationale | Introduction | Complete |
| 3. Objectives | Research questions | Complete |

| 5. Setting | Methods 2.2 | Synthetic setting only |
| 6. Participants | Methods 2.2 | Synthetic generation described |
| 7. Variables | Methods 2.5 and data dictionary | Complete |
| 8. Data sources/measurement | Methods 2.2-2.5 | Complete for synthetic pipeline |
| 9. Bias | Methods 2.6; Limitations | Partial; no real field design |
